## Introduction

ConvertKit is an email marketing service built specifically for bloggers and content creators. This can help bloggers and content creators engage their audience and drive more traffic to their Elegant web application.

## Installation & Setup

ConvertKit is automatically configured with all new Elegant applications so you may start using it immediately. To learn how to create a new Elegant application, please consult Elegant's [installation documentation](/docs/installation).

<a name="convertkit_install_into_elegant"></a>

### Installing ConvertKit into your Elegant application.

If you are interested in using ConvertKit with your Elegant application, you may simply provide an action url string to the `NEXT_PUBLIC_CONVERTKIT_ACTION_URL` environment variable in your `.env` file. 

```ini
NEXT_PUBLIC_CONVERTKIT_ACTION_URL="https://app.convertkit.com/forms/21224410/subscriptions"
```

### Finding your ConvertKit Action URL

To find your ConvertKit Action Url, navigate to your [ConverKit dashboard](https://app.convertkit.com/) and click on `Landing Pages & Forms`.

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_dashboard.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

Next click `Create new` at the bottom righthand side of the Forms page.

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_forms_page.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

Now click `Form` on the next page.

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_newform.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

You will be asked to choose the display format of your form. Please select `Inline`.

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_form_layout.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

Next click the "Mills" newsletter template from the list of available templates. 

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_mills.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

Finally, click `Save`, and then `Publish` in the top right hand corner of the page. A modal will popup with a few different tabs.
Select `HTML`, and your Action URL will be listed in the provided HTML code.

<div className="my-8 shadow-xl">
  <img className="rounded-xl" src={convertkit_action_url.src} alt="ConvertKit and Elegant together make it easy to grow your email subscribers lists"/>
</div>

### Saving your Action URL to Elegant

Copy the form url from the provided action handler, and paste it into your `.env` file.

```none
NEXT_PUBLIC_CONVERTKIT_ACTION_URL="https://app.convertkit.com/forms/5018207/subscriptions"
```
